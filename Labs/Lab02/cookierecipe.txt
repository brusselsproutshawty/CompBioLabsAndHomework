Yummy chocolate chip cookie recipe

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Whisk 2.25 cupps all purpose flour, 1.25 teaspoons kosher salt and 1 teaspoon baking soda in a bowl. 
In a separate bowl, cream 1 cup unsalted butter, 1 cup white sugar and 0.5 cup light brown sugar together until mixture is light and fluffy.
Add 1 large egg, 2 tablespoons milk and 1.25 teaspoons vanilla extract. 
Pour dry ingredients into the wet ingredients.
Stir until flour is thoroughly mixed.
Stir in chocolate chips.
Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
Scoop out rounded tablespoons of dough and place on prepared baking sheet, leaving 4 inches of space between cookies. 
Bake in preheated oven for 12 minutes or until cookies are golden brown. 
Slide parchment and cookies onto a cooling rack.
Once cooled, enjoy.
